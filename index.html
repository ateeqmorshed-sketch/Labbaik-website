Our Story

15 years of teaching Calgary to drive — one patient lesson at a time

Labbaik Driving School was founded by Abdul Hannan and has spent the past 15 years serving Calgarians of every age and background. What started as one instructor's commitment to road safety has grown into a school that's watched hundreds of nervous beginners become confident, self-assured drivers.

Our instructors meet every student where they are. New to driving or simply working on specific skills, we plan each lesson around how you actually learn — not a one-size-fits-all script. The goal isn't just passing a test; it's building the lifelong habits of a defensive driver who's genuinely safer on the road.

Safety and confidence isn't just a priority for us — it's our promise. — The Labbaik Driving School team

Our Approach

What every lesson is built around

Four things guide how we teach, from your very first lesson to the day you drive off on your own.

01

Patience first

Every student learns differently. Our instructors slow down, explain clearly, and never rush the process.

02

Defensive by design

We teach hazard awareness and proactive habits that protect you long after the road test is over.

03

Real city driving

Lessons happen on Calgary's actual streets and highways, so you're ready for the roads you'll really drive.

04

A plan, not a guess

Each session builds on the last, so you can see your own progress and know exactly what's next.
